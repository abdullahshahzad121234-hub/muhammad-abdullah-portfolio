Alia USA

A full-fledged organization website built with Webflow CMS. Designed to be clean, trustworthy, and easy to manage — empowering the client to take full control of their content.

Live Website View Tech Stack

Project Overview

Alia USA is a professional organization that needed a complete digital presence. The goal was to create a website that reflects their mission, builds trust with their audience, and provides an easy way for the team to manage content without technical assistance.

User Experience

I focused on creating an intuitive navigation structure that makes it easy for visitors to find what they need. The design uses clean layouts, strategic whitespace, and clear calls-to-action.

The Challenge

The client had no coding experience and needed a website they could update independently. The site required complex multi-level navigation, dynamic content sections, and a blog/news system.

My Solution

I designed the UI in Figma, then built the site in Webflow using a modular CMS structure with reusable components. This allows the client to add/edit content easily without touching code.
