Tremco Construction

A fast, modern construction website built with Webflow, Lenis smooth scroll, and GSAP animations. Delivering a premium experience for industrial clients.

Live Website View Tech Stack

Project Overview

Tremco Construction is an industrial construction company that needed a modern digital presence to showcase their portfolio of large-scale projects.

User Experience

I designed a user journey that guides visitors from the hero section through project showcases and finally to contact. Smooth scrolling and subtle animations keep users engaged.

The Problem

The client's previous website was outdated, slow, and difficult to navigate. Heavy imagery caused lag, especially on mobile devices.

The Solution

I rebuilt the site from scratch in Webflow, implementing Lenis for buttery-smooth scroll and GSAP for lightweight animations. All images were optimized and lazy-loaded.
